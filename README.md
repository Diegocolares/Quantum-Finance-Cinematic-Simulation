## Quantum Finance Cinematic Simulation

![Quantum Finance Animation](assets/finanças.gif)


This project presents a cinematic visualization of financial markets inspired by concepts from quantum mechanics. Instead of modeling price as a deterministic trajectory, the system represents market dynamics as a probabilistic state evolving over time, incorporating uncertainty, regime behavior, and scenario superposition.

The animation is structured in three conceptual phases. First, the market is observed as a continuous state with an uncertainty band, analogous to a quantum distribution. Then, multiple future trajectories emerge simultaneously, representing the coexistence of possible market regimes. Finally, a collapse occurs, selecting a single realized outcome, mimicking the measurement process in quantum systems.

The visual engine combines synthetic market data, dynamic uncertainty modeling, and multi-scenario branching to produce a high-impact, cinematic GIF. Glow effects, temporal transitions, and particle-based background elements are used to reinforce the interpretation of markets as complex, non-deterministic systems.

This project is designed not only as a visualization tool but also as a conceptual bridge between quantitative finance and modern statistical physics frameworks, including non-extensive entropy, stochastic processes, and regime detection.

---

## Key Concepts

* Probabilistic market representation instead of deterministic forecasting
* Superposition of future financial scenarios
* Dynamic uncertainty modeling (volatility as a state distribution)
* Collapse mechanism as realization of a market outcome
* Cinematic data visualization for scientific communication
